🧠 Automation Rules

💡 Important Clarification

Automations are created based on installed devices.
You cannot build automations without sensors, switches, or inputs in place.

Each system includes 2 basic automations.

  • Motion → Light ON
  • No motion → Light OFF (with delay)
  • Optional time-based behavior
